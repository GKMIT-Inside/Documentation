# **Business Cases**

This section describes the business value and strategic implications of **GKMIT_INSIDE** features.

## Knowledge Centralization

### Business Value

Creating a single source of truth for organisational knowledge within the GKMIT community.

### Key Benefits

**Reduced Redundancy**

- Eliminates repeated questions about solved problems
- Decreases time spent explaining concepts multiple times
- Minimizes duplicate work on similar challenges

**Improved Efficiency**

- New team members onboard faster with accessible documentation
- Developers find solutions without interrupting colleagues
- Knowledge persists beyond individual team members

### Measurable Outcomes

- Reduction in time-to-resolution for common issues
- Decrease in repeat questions in communication channels
- Faster onboarding time for new developers
